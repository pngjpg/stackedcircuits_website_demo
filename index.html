Stacked Circuits Changes 3D Printing and Circuit Design

Turn any FDM 3D printer into your own circuit board factory

100% Metal

Stacked Circuits has the only 100% metal 3D printing filament on the market, no binders or additives.

No Post Processing

By simply printing metal, do debinding or sintering steps are required! Parts are functional right after printing finishes.

300x More Conductive

With a resistivity of about 15 μΩ cm, our material approaches the conductivity of copper while maintaining printability.

Lower Cost

Reduce downtime waiting for prototype boards, making your teams more efficient.

Fewer Mistakes

Unlock iterative design for electronics, allowing more prototypes for the same time and budget.

Faster Development

Electronics design can now keep up with physical product development, bringing integrated systems to market faster.
