Circuit Design Is Stuck In The Past

Stacked Circuits brings the speed and flexibility of 3D printing to the electronics prototyping industry. Our patent pending metal 3D printing filament turns any off the shelf filament-based 3D printer into a circuit board factory.

Cloud computing has accelerated the pace of AI, 3D printing revolutionized mechanical design, but circuit board manufacturing is still stuck with tech from the 1900s.

How It Works

  • Design Your Circuit

    Design a circuit in your favorite EDA software. Any software is compatible, just export a Gerber file.

  • Convert to a 3D Model

    Upload your gerber file to stackedcircuits.com and convert it to a 3D printable model.

  • 3D Print

    Slice and print on almost any FDM 3D printer, no modifications necessary!

Why Print Your Circuits?

300 times more conductive than the best commercial alternative, Stacked Circuits has the only product on the market that prints as 100% metal, requiring no post processing, sintering, or binder removal steps. Better yet, our material is compatible with any FDM 3D printer: just click print and make 3D printed circuits in minutes.

Faster circuits mean fewer mistakes. Unlock iterative design and rapid prototyping concepts for electronics, reducing the need for bodge wires and repairs.
Faster circuits mean faster design. Get products to market faster, accelerating development and reacting to market demand before competitors.
Faster circuits mean lower cost. Reduce downtime waiting for prototype boards, making your teams more efficient.
